Florence

During our visit to Florence we will be visiting numerous palaces as well as the Piazzale Michelangelo, San Miniato al Monte, the Basilica (pictured) and a number of famous gardens including Oricellari and Boboli.

One day will be left for everyone to relax in the glorious weather or to visit an alternative attraction of their choice. The tour guide will be on hand to offer advice on availability and organising of any of these tours.

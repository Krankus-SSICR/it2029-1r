Paris

Our visit to paris will begin with a bus tour of the city. We will be leaving the bus in a number of places to visit Champs-Elysees, the Louvre, Notre Dame amongst other places.

The second day of our visit will begin with a visit to the Eiffel tower, followed by a boat ride along the Rover Senne. Guests will also be offered the opportunity to purchase tickets for a Moulin Rouge Paris Dinner and Show.

The third day of our visit will incorporate a vist to Versaille and Giverny with the highlights being a visit to Versaille Palace and Monet's Garden.

Our last day in Paris will be left for shopping or individual activities.
